Innovative Thinking

Employed throughout the design

At Rocket Elevator our team of engineers are always seeking out innovations that will enhance our products. The Rocket Elevator is evidence of that. It’s one of the only few elevators of its kind that comes equipped with either a manual accordion gate or fully-automatic sliding doors. It is powered by our fluitronic drive system which has the added option of utilizing environmentally friendly synthetic bio-degradable fluids. Unlike many other home elevators, the Rocket Elevator does not draw power when it’s sitting idle. And to extend the green story all Rocket Elevator home elevators are equipped with LED lights.
